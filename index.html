What is Nostr?

Notes and other stuff transmitted by relays

Nostr flips the current internet paradigm of "dumb client/smart server" to "smart client/dumb server", by using relays and public-key crytography. Nostr empowers users to control their own data.

Be your own content curator

Interact with people from across the globe and discover your niche communities. Without a forced closed-source algorithm, your home feed is based on your follows and interests.

No central server

Alice can send her notes to any servers of her choice.

Bob can run a server and set his own rules. People can use his relay if they like his relay services, or just fetch specific notes.

Learn about decentralization

Signed at the core

Instead of using insecure passwords, all messages are signed using cryptography. You can verify messages & user authenticity without relying on a centralized component. You own your nostr.

#asknostr

Can anyone develop on Nostr?

YES! Nostr's super-power is its permissionless development enviroment. As an interopable and modular protocol, Nostr is a dream to develop for.

What's with the Ostrich?

The Ostrich is the beloved mascot of Nostr users.

What are NIPs?

NIPs stand for Nostr Implementation Possibilities. They are standards for implementing required or optional features through the Nostr protocol.

Why are browser clients considered unsafe?

This is a valid concern, but web clients are safe as long as they ensure to code against regular attacks such as Cross Site Scripting. For web developers this can be easily mitigated with a strict content safety policy. Regardless of what client you are using, you should always make sure you trust the author of the software.
